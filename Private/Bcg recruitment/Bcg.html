Job Responsibilities:

As a Workflow Associate, you will coordinate DRS research workflow across 1-2 industries with customer service excellence, accuracy, and organization. You will manage the daily workflow with an increasing level of independence, making sure capacities are managed optimally and, in a business-focused manner. You will set the tone and expectation for the customer, from beginning to assigning of the work.


Eligibility Criteria:

  • Master/Bachelor or advanced degree in quality assurance, software engineering, computer science, or related disciplines

Preferred skill:

  • 0-1 years of relevant experience and general interest in business research
  • Bachelor’s degree with demonstrated high academic achievement
  • Experience with customer service in a fast-paced environment is very desirable
  • Experience in secondary research is desirable
  • Proficiency in Excel, Word, and PowerPoint
  • Well-developed communication skills in English

Additional Inforamation :

The Data and Research Services (DRS) function is a dynamic and highly visible center of excellence focused on business research and process innovation. We collaborate with our consulting teams to bring advanced research capabilities to support projects and clients. DRS provides specialist research services to consulting teams and our Knowledge Team, taking deep pride in delivering rapid and precise results.

DRS works with both qualitative and quantitative data, conducting research and providing synthesized results from over 100 commercial sources, publicly available data, company and industry research, macro-economic, commodities, and trade data.
