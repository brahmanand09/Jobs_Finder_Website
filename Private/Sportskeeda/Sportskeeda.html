Sportskeeda Work From Home for Static Writers |12th Pass

Company Name Sportskeeda
Post Name Static Writers
Salary up to ₹3.6 LPA*
Experience 0-1 yr
Batch 2023/22/21/20/19/18/17
Job Location Work From Home
Website www.sportskeeda.com

Job Responsibilities:

  • Write 2-3 static articles daily a minimum of 800-1000 words (teams, player pages, tournament pages, etc).
  • Able to work under deadlines and deliver work quickly and efficiently.
  • Establishes standards, guidelines and process tailoring to be followed in projects
  • Articles should not have an ounce of plagiarism, falsified facts, idea theft, clout-farming, click-baiting, and should not be derogatory in any form.
  • Expected to develop a knowledge base for trends, news, fresh content from creators etc.
  • Should be able to write about the players, their life, NFL career, etc.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Any graduate degree or equivalent with a strong academic record from any stream.

Preferred skill:

  • Good communication and analytical skills.
  • Effective probing skills and analyzing / understanding skills
  • Analytical skills with customer-centric approach
  • Excellent proficiency with written English and with a neutral English accent
  • Should be able to work on a flexible schedule (including weekend shifts)
  • Having a passing knowledge of other popular US sports like NBA, MBL, etc. would be a bonus.

Requirements:

  • Cover Letter.
  • Past work samples (if Any).
  • Relevant experience in writing and editing.
  • Decent understanding of the sport and its stats.
  • A solid grasp of the English language and the ability to communicate niche ideas to a
  • wide-spread audience.
